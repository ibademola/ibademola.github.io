Welcome to my page

I am passionate about research that enhances our understanding of urban environments, particularly in managing the adverse effects of both anthropogenic and natural events, including rapid urbanization and unprecedented population growth. By leveraging the incredible technologies of remote sensing and machine learning, we aim to ensure sustainable urban development. My research focuses on:

  • Thermal Remote Sensing
  • Forest and vegetation remote sensing
  • Coastal management
  • Climate change studies
  • Machine Learning and AI
